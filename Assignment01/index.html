Our Story

Urban Interiors takes pride in being a 4th generation family owned business. The company was founded in 1946 by the current owner's grandfather, who started out selling sundries in Pike Place Market.

For over 75+ years the Moscatel Family has been selling and designing furniture in the greater Seattle area. The company has grown with an incredible team that is deeply committed to continuing to grow their grandfather's original furniture business.
